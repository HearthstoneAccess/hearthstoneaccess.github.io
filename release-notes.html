November 26 2021: 21.8.0.126451-17

  • Book of Heroes Valeera is now available
  • Cursed effects on cards are now narrated as they happen and are described when reading a card
  • You can now read your mana when discovering and choosing cards
  • Fixed a bug introduced in a recent patch where tradeable cards weren't being narrated properly when traded
  • Fixed a bug where shuffled cards such as Ignite and tradeable cards would be narrated when redrawn by your opponent even though you're not supposed to know which card was being drawn
  • Fixed a bug with cards such as Stealer of Souls where cards would be incorrectly read as costing mana instead of health
  • Fixed a bug where cards such as Guess the Weight would be incorrectly narrated when played by your opponent
  • Fixed a bug where opening the My Collection screen in Battlegrounds or Mercenaries would crash the game so players using OCR or getting sighted help can access them
  • Fixed a bug where cards summoned straight from your deck into the battlefield would be narrated as if they were still hidden
  • Fixed a rare bug where your minions would immediately attack once a turn started if you had used Shift+F to send all minions to your opponent's face in the previous turn but ran out of time before everyone could attack
  • Fixed a rare bug where the entire set up phase of a Book of Heroes mission such as shuffling your deck with 30 cards and drawing new ones for the mulligan phase would sometimes be narrated after conceding and immediately restarting a game
  • Languages which don't require sentence ending periods such as Thai can now explicitly stop periods from being added to sentences and read over TTS
  • Added additional defensive code and error messages to help track down a rare bug with return to hand cards such as Shadowstep
